**Welcome to the Create Event**

Hey adventurers, are you ready for a unique public create event? From June 28th you can participate on our event server at create.crystopia.net.
We invite you to become part of a community that focuses on building the best bases and realizing creative ideas.

Our modpack is already available on our Discord and will soon also be available on Curseforge. Look forward to exciting adventures and the opportunity to live out and improve your artistic skills.

## Features

### Claim

![Create-Claim](/images/Create-Images/claims.png)

- Claim your territories and build your factories. You can also invite your friends with `/guild` and then build together. Or use the HQ Function to set a Guild Spawn!

### Artifacts

![Create-Artifacts](/images/Create-Images/artifacts.png)

- Collect artifacts to make yourself stronger and gain more skills.

### Backpacks

![Create-Backpacks](/images/Create-Images/backpacks.png)

- Craft backpacks and easily improve them with materials to store your items.

### Chunk Loader

![Create-Chunk Loader](/images/Create-Images/Chunk-loader.png)

- Craft chunk loaders to keep your factories charged and produce more things.

### Copy Cats

![Create-Copy Cats](/images/Create-Images/Copy-cats.png)

- Craft copy cats and use blocks as textures to be more creative.

### Train stations

![Create-Train stations](/images/Create-Images/db-service.png)

- Create better train networks and connect them to the DB service.

### Diesel generators & electricity

![Create-Diesel generators](/images/Create-Images/Defaul-create.png)
![Create-Electricity](/images/Create-Images/electric.png)

- Generate large amounts of energy and use it for your factories.

### Economy Trade

![Create-Economy Trade](/images/Create-Images/Eco-system.png)

- Create shops, sell items to players and earn Create Coins.

### Crafts

![Create-Handcraft](/images/Create-Images/handcraftet.png)

- Craft cool decorative items and design your base with great items.
